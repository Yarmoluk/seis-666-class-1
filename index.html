3. Dan McCreary: Intelligent Textbook Workshop [01:33-02:19]

Guest Speaker Background: Former head of AI at UnitedHealthcare, Bell Labs, Next Computer employee #50, worked with Steve Jobs

Core Concepts:

A. Exponential Growth Demonstration [01:36-01:42]

  • Moore's Law MicroSIM: Shows exponential transistor growth on log scale
  • AI Doubling Rate: Task horizon doubling since 2019
  • 2030 Projection: By 2030, AI could complete 500-day human tasks in minutes with 50% accuracy
  • Critical Question: "If you don't believe this, your strategy will be different than if you do believe it"

B. Digital Nervous System = Knowledge Graphs [01:42-02:13]

  • Definition: Your digital strategy is a model of your customers, products, competitors, and competitors' products
  • Problem with Legacy Systems: SAP implementations are flat, designed for counting/amounts, NOT for prediction
  • Neuro-Symbolic AI: Combining LLMs (probabilistic) with symbolic models (structured) creates superior results
  • Similarity as Superpower: AI's ability to find similar products, customers, complaints, errors, social posts unlocks new analysis

C. MicroSIMs (Micro-Simulations) [01:35-01:37]

  • Simple enough for AI to generate, interactive enough to monitor student engagement
  • Use feedback to predict next learning steps
  • Examples: Moore's Law visualization, closest point finder in calculus
  • All generated by AI with minimal tweaking

D. The Intelligent Textbook Process [01:52-02:13]

  1. Start with ONE PAGE course description
  2. Run through Bloom's Taxonomy for pedagogical structure
  3. Generate knowledge graph (200+ concepts with relationships)
  4. Auto-create: chapters, quizzes, glossaries, FAQs, MicroSIMs
  5. Result: Complete textbook in 2 days (Dan's calculus book: 1,000 pages, 130 microsims)

E. Product Manager as Future's Most Critical Role [02:06-02:13]

  • Most Important Skill: EMPATHY (understanding customer pain)
  • New Workflow: 15 windows running concurrently - 10 for planning, 5 for coding
  • Focus: Managing 100+ Claude agents constantly improving your product
  • Success Formula: Empathy + Product Focus + Mental Model of Agent Systems
